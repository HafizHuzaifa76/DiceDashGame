Welcome to Dice Dash Game

Challenge friends or play against the computer in this exciting dice-rolling game!

Game Rules:

  • Two players take turns to roll a dice.
  • You can roll as many times as you want during your turn.
  • If you roll a 1, your turn ends, and you lose all points for that turn.
  • You can "Give Up" to save your points and end your turn.
  • The first player to score 100 wins!

Key Features:

  • 2-Player Mode: Challenge a friend in an exciting dice battle!
  • Player vs Computer Mode: Test your skills against a challenging AI opponent.
  • Unlock New Dice: Earn coins in the game and unlock new, exciting dice!
  • Simple and Engaging Gameplay: Easy to learn, hard to master, for all ages!
  • Compete for the Top Spot: First to 100 points wins. Are you the best?
